Your Ask

The ask consists in demonstrating your ability the explain the performance of NANR as a function of the other variables in using a multivariate regression framework.

In a concise .Rmd (i.e. no flexdashboards), follow the workflow in the class note module on multivariate regressions.

  1. Extract data from 2016-01-01 to 2020-09-30 and compute daily log() returns. It is not necessary to display in your document as this is the data wrangling of it.
  2. Provide a correlation matrix and/or pairs plot and comment on whether you see potential issues with multicolinearity.
  3. Compute regressions by recursively adding independent variables.
  4. Compute the Variance Inflation Factor (“VIF”) for all your regressions.
  5. Make a clear recommendation on what multivariate regression you deem best and the reasons why. Use all the knowledge you gained on regressions up to this point in class.
